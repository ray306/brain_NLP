Experimental procedure

Our EEG experiment adopted a canonical two-word priming paradigm, with stimuli visually presented to the participants. We used 240 two-character Chinese nouns as stimuli (an analogous English version is also carried out). We randomly form 120 Prime-Target pairs.

Quantify the neural measures of semantic similarity by the responses to the target in the target trial minus those in the prime trial (same word with different preceding contexts)
