Brain-NLP database

The EEG datasets are stored on OSF

Currently we have EEG data of Chinese word pairs. We are obtaining the English version.

We cordially invite cognitive neuroscientists to contribute datasets in more languages. Please contact corresponding authors and discuss.

Currently, we have test three representative NLP models:

  • CBOW (Continuous Bag-of-words) model (Mikolov et al. 2013)

  • GloVe (Global Vectors) model (Pennington et al. 2014)

  • CWE (Character-enhanced Word embedding) model (Chen et al. 2015)

We welcome computer scientists to test more NLP models with our EEG datasets.
