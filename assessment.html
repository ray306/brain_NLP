Online human-like assessment for NLP models

Under construction

We aim to build an online assessment platform. Researchers only need to upload the semantic similarity measures generated by your models for the pre-defined word pairs. An objective index of how human-like semantic representation can be automatically obtained.

Currently, researchers can download the EEG datasets and perform the correlational analysis.
